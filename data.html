Collected Data

Environmental data was collected including temperature, humidity, and pest activity levels. Data analysis revealed a strong correlation between high humidity and increased maize weevil activity.

Key Findings

  • Humidity above 65% significantly promotes weevil infestation.
  • Temperature fluctuations also contribute to pest breeding cycles.
  • IoT real-time monitoring detected early infestations before visible damage occurred.
