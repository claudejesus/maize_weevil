Research Background

Post-harvest maize losses due to weevil infestations threaten food security. Traditional methods are costly and environmentally risky. IoT technology offers a modern solution by providing real-time monitoring and early detection, reducing pesticide reliance, and improving storage conditions.

Project Objectives

  • To design and implement an IoT-based monitoring system.
  • To develop automated responses against maize weevil infestations.
  • To analyze and compare effectiveness with traditional methods.

Research Questions

  • How can IoT technology improve pest monitoring?
  • What environmental factors can predict weevil infestations?
  • How does IoT performance compare with conventional methods?
